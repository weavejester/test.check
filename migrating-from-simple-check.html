Migrating from simple-check

In order to migrate from simple-check to test.check, you’ll need to do two things:

  • Update project.clj

    In your project.clj replace [reiddraper/simple-check "0.5.6"] with [org.clojure/test.check "0.6.2"] (note: your version numbers may be different).

  • Update namespace declarations

    Update your namespaces: simple-check.core becomes clojure.test.check (note the dropping of ‘core’). For everything else you can simply replace simple-check with clojure.test.check. Let’s make it easy:

    find test -name '*.clj' -print0 | xargs -0 sed -i.bak \
-e 's/simple-check.core/clojure.test.check/' \
-e 's/simple-check/clojure.test.check/'

    Review the updates.
